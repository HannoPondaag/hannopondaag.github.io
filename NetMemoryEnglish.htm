    The first requirement is that two computers, with two instances of the software, have an internet connection. When you are Player #1, for example, on your computer, WIFI is on.
    There are multiple configurations possible like a multiplayer (2 players) game or Solo, but a 2 Players game via a Network is my favourite !

    Case #1

    [Connect]-ing two computers in a (Wireless) home network situation using KPN Modem Sagemcom box B12

    First you may want to know, when a connection is established between two instances of such a Memory Game X. You can touch, or click with your mouse, let's say 'teleport' Game Play clicks like this, in the following manner:
    Player #1 turns on UPnP IGD in the KPN Sagemcom Experia box B12 Modem/Router. UPnP IGD must be enabled in the modem settings due to... the security ? It is possible for gaming according to the KPN (provider) forum.
    Make a rule for a Memory Game x game or a Net Four In A Row version in the Modem and make sure it is turned on.
    Save the just made changes.
    Let's say: you are Player #1, your opponent Player #2.
    Then Player #1 is searching, using the Command-prompt 'CMD' and ipconfig, the host address of his or her computer 1 for filling in the internal host in the Modem Settings.
    In a field 'external host' of the Modem of Player #1, with Port forwarding, the IPV4 item should be recognized by an asterisk typographical symbol or star-shaped character: '*'.

    Player #1 is searching, using the Command-prompt and ipconfig, the external host address then, this will Player #2 fill in in the inputfield of his or her or program later, see below.
    Then if the data in the Modem was saved, Player #2 touches or presses or clicks, with his or her mousepointer [Listen].
    Player #1 is typing in his or her inputfield the host address of computer 2 of Player #2 found by Player #2, which he or her should find, searching with the Command-prompt 'CMD' and ipconfig, this address of his or her computer he/she should remembering from a note or by head, It is one specific IP address from the private network addresses in the 192.168.xxx.xxx range, like for example 192.168.2.7 in my case.
    Player #2 is, does the same things mirror wise, clicking [Listen] typing in the inputfield of the program on computer 2, the external host IP address already found by player #1.
    Once [Enter] is touched or pressed with the mousepointer, Player #1 is counting 3..2..1.. & Player #1 touches or clicks with the mousepointer [Connect], after this 1 of 2 sec. approximately later. Player #2 should do the same. Mind the order of actions described, VPN off for both and the last clicking of the [Connect] button below the IP address inputfield within the 30 seconds time out ? You did it.
    Result: Test Succeeded ! Connection established.
    Now you just have to Start a New Game one from Memory Game X and don't forget clicking next [Send]. Clicking that button once, will be sending the Game Play data for the Net Memory Game Series Premium program, Memory Game X, one complete (hidden) set of cards from the server side to the client side across the network. Or once the same thing should be applied vice versa, in the opposite direction, on the other system thus.
    Using Full Screen, with the right resoltion of the screen, start Playing the Game next !

    Case #4

    [Connect]-ing two computers (this can be probably achieved over the WAN (internet) also). One with KPN MODEM Sagemcom Experia box B12 with a MS Windows tablet computer via fiber and Wi-Fi hotspot to the other, a laptop, through an Android 9 mobile smartphone in home network situation (in this way over the internet is working also to your family, Friend or aquaintance!) for inter-connecting the data of the Game Play to and from the tablet for the Game to a laptop instance of the Game:
    Make sure that your resolution is set in such away that all elements fall within the frame, and that there is no overlap between the input field and 'the blue rack' or any elements in the Four In A Row version.
    'Player #1', in Access Control-->Portforwarding--, has turned UPnP IGD on again in the KPN Sagemcom Experia box B12 Modem/Router..
    ..
    Create a separate rule for a Memory Game x game or a Four-in-a-Row version in the Modem in the manner described in Case #1.
    Create a hotspot on your mobile smartphone over WiFi & connect the computer /tablet of 'Player #2' via the mobile smartphone over WiFi, the smartphone itself serves as a Wifi point (e.g. temporarily during the procedure of building up a connection and while playing later)..
    Next my 'opponent', 'Player #2' is looking up his/her IP host address op via..
    Automatic [DHCP] was ON. An IP address from the: Private Network non-overlapping range 192.168.xxx.xxx on his or her device..
    Look into (i) on your hotspot on your computer or tablet. Of you click (i), Network and Internet will appear. There you'll find the IP4 Address of your hotspot to your mobile. Click (i) --> Network & Internet
    (here comes an image of device #2 of 'Player #2' screenshot)..

    'Player #1' counts 3..2..1.. if he or she has pressed or clicked [Connect] with his or her mouse pointer.
    'Player #2' also presses or clicks [Connect] (with his/her mouse pointer) shortly afterwards, e.g. 1 to 2 seconds later.
